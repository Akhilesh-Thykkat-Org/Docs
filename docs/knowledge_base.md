# WhatsApp Business API – Knowledge Base

## 1. What is WhatsApp Business API?

WhatsApp Business API (WABA) is a communication solution designed for medium and large businesses to communicate with customers on WhatsApp at scale.

Unlike the WhatsApp Business App, the API is built for **automation, integrations, and multi-user access**.

### Key Highlights

- No mobile phone required  
- Works via an approved WhatsApp Business Solution Provider  
- Supports automation and chatbots  
- Enables CRM, website, and internal system integrations  
- Built for compliance and scalability  

---

## 2. WhatsApp Business App vs WhatsApp Business API

| Feature | WhatsApp Business App | WhatsApp Business API |
|--------|------------------------|------------------------|
| Requires phone | Yes | No |
| Multi-user support | No | Yes |
| Automation | No | Yes |
| CRM integration | No | Yes |
| Green tick verification | No | Yes |
| Best suited for | Small businesses | Growing & enterprise businesses |

✅ If you need automation, multiple agents, or system integrations, the API is the right choice.

---

## 3. How WhatsApp Business API Works (Simple Explanation)

Here’s the simplified flow:

1. Your business gets approved for WhatsApp Business API  
2. Your phone number is connected to a WhatsApp platform  
3. Customers send messages to your WhatsApp number  
4. Messages appear in a shared dashboard  
5. Agents or bots reply from one system  

Once live, **no physical phone is needed**.

## 4. WhatsApp Conversation Types Explained

WhatsApp classifies conversations to protect users from spam and ensure quality.

---

### Service Conversations

- Initiated by the customer  
- Free-form replies allowed  
- 24-hour reply window  

**Examples:**

- Customer support  
- Product questions  
- Order-related queries  

---

### Business-Initiated Conversations (Templates)

- Initiated by the business  
- Requires approved message templates  
- Used for notifications and alerts  

**Examples:**

- Order confirmations  
- Appointment reminders  
- Delivery updates  
- Payment alerts  

---

## 5. What Are WhatsApp Message Templates?

Message templates are pre-approved messages that businesses can send outside the 24-hour customer reply window.

### Template Categories

- **Utility** – Order updates, reminders  
- **Authentication** – OTPs and verification  
- **Marketing** – Promotional messages (requires explicit opt-in)  

### Rules

- Must follow WhatsApp content policies  
- Reviewed before approval  
- Can include variables, buttons, and links  

---

## 6. What Is Opt-In and Why Is It Important?

Opt-in means the customer has given permission to receive messages from your business.

### Valid Opt-In Methods

- Website forms with consent checkbox  
- WhatsApp “Click to Chat” buttons  
- Customer-initiated messages  
- Verbal consent (must be recorded)  

### Important Notes

- Messaging without opt-in can lead to warnings or suspension  
- Marketing messages always require opt-in  
- Opt-in must be clear and transparent  

---

## 7. WhatsApp Green Tick (Official Business Account)

The green tick indicates that WhatsApp has verified your business as an Official Business Account.

### Benefits of Green Tick

- Increased trust and credibility  
- Higher open and response rates  
- Brand name shown instead of phone number  

### Approval Depends On

- Business verification status  
- Brand recognition  
- Message quality and compliance

## 9. Is WhatsApp Business API Paid?

Yes. WhatsApp charges **per conversation**, not per message.

### Pricing Depends On

- Conversation type (Service, Utility, Marketing, Authentication)  
- Customer’s country  
- Messaging volume  

Most platforms provide:

- Usage tracking  
- Cost visibility  
- Analytics and reports  
- Tools to reduce conversation costs

## 10. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Can I use my existing WhatsApp number?

Yes, as long as the number is not currently active on a WhatsApp app.

---

### Can multiple agents reply at the same time?

Yes. The API supports multiple agents simultaneously.

---

### Can I integrate WhatsApp with my website or CRM?

Yes. WhatsApp Business API supports APIs and webhooks for integrations.

---

### Is WhatsApp Business API secure?

Yes. Messages are end-to-end encrypted and policy-compliant.
